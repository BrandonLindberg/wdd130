About Me

My name is Brandon Lindberg. This is my personal website, where you can see who I am, what I’ve done, and what I am planning to do.

I grew up in Eastern Washington, and graduated from Cheney High School in 2020. I have been interested in computers from a young age, ever since we got a PS2. I started messing around with making games in middle school. I have been using Unity game engine for a long time, but recently I have started using Unreal Engine 5. I enjoy modeling things in Blender, and I have experience with Python, HTML, CSS, C#, and C++. I also enjoy building computers and messing with them. I am currently attending BYU Idaho with my fiancé.
